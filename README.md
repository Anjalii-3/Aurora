### Project Description
A warm, friendly study companion website that helps students when they feel low about academics.  
Students enter their mood and topic to get gentle encouragement, simple explanations, and a daily boost.

### The Problem statement
Many students feel demotivated, overwhelmed, or stuck while studying.  
When this happens, they struggle to start or continue learning, even for simple topics.

### The Solution
We provide a lightweight web app that combines short, human-like encouragement with simple topic explanations and a daily motivational boost, helping students regain confidence and continue studying.
